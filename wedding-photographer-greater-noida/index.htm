Things To Keep In Mind While Looking For A Wedding Photographer In Greater Noida

Finding the best and professional wedding photographer is tough, but the process can become easy if you keep in mind the following factors before booking them.

  1. Start Early: In India, on a particular day or auspicious day, hundreds of marriage ceremonies are performed in different households during the marriage season. Better you start your search for best photographers early for your special day.
  2. Meet Them In Person: Before you decide and make advance payment online to a wedding photographer, it’s best to meet him/her in person. You can shortlist a few based on an online portfolio, meet them at a coffee shop, clear all the doubts, and select the best one.
  3. Negotiate on Budget: You should not be afraid of negotiating a budget. It’s a good practice as it helps you understand how much effort a wedding photographer has to put into getting candid and memorable images. And it helps the photographer understand your expectations. Most photographers customize wedding photography packages as per couple’s specific requirements.
  4. Quality over Quantity: When it comes to having a keepsake of the best moments you had on your special day, it’s best to go with Quality over Quantity. Instead of having an album filled with images, you must agree for quality images as the best moment of your d-day.

Questions To Must Ask Wedding Photographers Before Booking

  1. Is my wedding date available in 2022?
  2. What is included in your standard package?
  3. Ask the wedding photography in Greater Noida charges beforehand. Let him know of your budget and your expectations.
  4. How big is your team?
  5. How many weddings do you do in a year?
  6. Are there any hidden charges?
  7. Discuss the taxes, advance payment, deliverables and other such aspects. You should also ask him about the number of people in his team, who will be accompanying him for all the events.
  8. How long have you been in this business? Can you share samples of work?
  9. Do you shoot both digital and film?
  10. Do you have a contract before we finalize?
  11. What type of album do you offer? Do you provide assistance selecting images and designing the album?
  12. What is the backup plan if you are unable to shoot my wedding for an unexpected reason?
  13. Let him know of the functions you would like him to cover such as mehndi, haldi, sangeet, wedding, reception etc., and what you are expecting from the coverage of each event. You will also need to tell him what kind of coverage you would like for each event such as still photography, videography or both.
  14. Do you include engagement photos in your packages? What about pre-wedding events like rehearsal dinners?
  15. What type of cameras do you use, and will you have any backups in case something breaks or doesn’t work?
  16. When you provide print albums, what type of paper and style will you use?
  17. Ask him about the date of delivery as well, and when should you expect the albums, etc. after the events are over.
  18. Do you charge a travel fee? For what distance?
