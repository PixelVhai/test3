Candid or Traditional Photography?

Wedding photographers in gurgaon go unconventional ways and capture candid emotions. The smallest spontaneous moments like when the groom catches the bride’s first glimpse come under candid pictures. One must go with candid photography over staged photographs captured in traditional ways to make the wedding album realistic.

Traditional Videography or Cinematography?

You want to be the lead or a star of your wedding. Doesn’t it? Cinematography over videography is about getting your wedding captured in storytelling ways, whereas videography is more about recording the proceedings. Both are significant in one and other ways. Wisely choose.

Wedding Invitation Videos Vs Teaser Vs Wedding Film?

[Checklist] Planning Your Wedding in Gurgaon? Keep A Checklist Handy To Avoid Last Minute Nuisance

The beauty of Indian weddings is that a couple can plan to get married any time of the year they like. No matter which time of the year you visit shops selling wedding trousseau, catering providers, banquet halls or wedding venues, and wedding photographers, you will find them in discussions with couples or their parents regarding their unique requirements for the wedding ceremonies. Delhi, Noida and Gurgaon, being the most popular and populous cities of India remain busy throughout the year accommodating to its customers.

As the best wedding photographers in gurgaon, we see things from our lenses. Hence, we want you to heed to our checklist exclusively prepared to help you avoid the huge rush common in peak wedding times and also to avoid last minute nuisance.

  1. Get the list of best muhurat from priest and fix your wedding date in advance
  2. Right after that decide your guests list. It helps you find the best banquet hall and negotiate the deal to make bookings
  3. Set your wedding budget to make best use of it on different ceremonies and personal expenditures
  4. Apply your file in advance for marriage registry
  5. Connect with vendors as soon as possible ( band, baghi, decorators, florist, caters, makeup artist, pandit, DJ, entertainer, invitation and others)
  6. Decide wedding dinner menu items list.
  7. Book your best photographer Book Important vendor Priority basis (Hire Priority Wedding Vendors)
  8. Book vehicles for pick up and drops on the wedding day.
  9. Prepare a list and start shopping for wedding trousseau
  10. Begin sending out the wedding invitations and inform relatives and friends in far away places on call so that they book flights or train tickets in advance
  11. Book the honeymoon tickets and complete all visa requirements
  12. Inform or apply leave in the office in advance
