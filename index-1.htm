Wedding Photography Package & Photoshoot Services

Indian weddings came a long way in 2022. From the past decade, Indians and Indian Shaadi have adopted several cultures, rituals, and people, but few things still remain the same such as traditional wedding videos and photographs. At The Wedding Focus, we have huge respect for diversity, and we value all the sentiments of every age group. Therefore, we have skillfully built our wedding photography packages and photoshoot services to meet all your requirements.

Traditional Photography -

Think it like old-school style photography with lots of “Smile, Please!” and classic photo of the bride with her arms around the groom in the exact angle composed by the photographer.

Candid photography -

It is the latest wedding photography style. All about candid – fewer poses, and more natural shots. Mostly, the person getting clicked doesn’t realize they’re on camera, so authentic emotions are caught. Eg. ✓ Bride getting ready, ✓ Romantic glance exchanged between couples.

Traditional Videography –

Remember those 1-2 hour long video with highlights of every wedding function of your parents and aunts? Traditional videography is you get what you saw in the marriage function. Minimal editing and raw romantic & upbeat Bollywood music play in the background.

Cinematic Videography -

It is a video file that seems like a movie on your own life. Crisp editing, fused with Bollywood and English music in the background, flip your entire wedding moments like a fairytale moment. Short clips of the couple expressing their feelings aloud and family. It also includes separate trailers & highlights of each function.

Pre-wedding photoshoot & video shoot -

This pre-wedding photo/video shoot lets the couple get captured in beautiful backdrops. It is a well-planned format of a photoshoot where the couples are set free to interact with each other and how they want away from wedding day chaos. check our pre wedding photography album

Drone wedding photography -

This trending technique might involve overhead shots of just the bride and groom or wedding party. Drone photography is a way to capture the spirit and excitement of the day in the bird-eye view. It adds images and videos in your album in different angles and vantage points, optimizing the appeal of many shots.

A Walk Through The Brief History and Culture of Delhi

The heart of the nation, the melting pot of the country, the magnificent lands that take us through the timelapse of the history of this country, the capital city of Delhi is a dream wedding destination for couples regardless of where they belong.

Inscribed with letters of gold, the rich culture and tradition of our capital state is not a secret for to-be married couples, as thousands of people fling to Delhi and its suburbs to tie the knot.

Surrounded by old Havelis, striking forts, vintage monuments, lusciously landscaped greenery, and well-planned urban parks, the capital city of Delhi dates back to the epic times of Mahabharata and has been linked as a thriving destination of some of India's oldest civilizations.


The myriad of places to get married in Delhi where the simply majestic archaeology of this state blurs as a backdrop of wedding ceremonies and receptions make it a favorite destination for professional photographers in India.

Wedding Culture of Delhi

The big fat Indian weddings that sweep the internet every time with their unmatched indulgence and extravagance are famous across the globe. And these big fat Indian weddings mostly have one common origin- the capital state of Delhi.


From the big and affluent North Indian families and internet personalities that join the bandwagon of lavish weddings to Bollywood celebrities and Social Media influencers who strive to make an entry into the 100 crore club, weddings in Delhi are always dazzled by the stupendous glamour, decorations, and hospitality.


The landscape of Delhi weddings is all about vibrant colors and rich traditions that are often played with fashion-forward statements and have been translated into innumerable ways including bringing in European Heritage or adding dashes of Hollywood drama.


Along with the breathtaking fireworks, crystal chandeliers, and luxurious feasts for the guests, weddings in Delhi are star-studded with palatial grandeur and never-before-seen indulgences.


From helicopters hovering over the wedding venue clicking pictures of ceremonies and receptions decking up wedding venues to weddings where two hundred chefs were flown in from India, Thailand, and Spain, we have seen the capital city of Delhi taking lavish weddings to a different level and we are eager to see more.
