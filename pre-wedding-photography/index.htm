Some of the Best Locations for Pre Wedding Photography in Delhi (2022)

There is something about the capital city of Delhi that leaves professional photographers from all around the world smitten with its stately architecture, majestic monuments, and breathtakingly beautiful urban parks. There are so many photo opportunities whether you are looking for photography options for a Delhi wedding or simply looking to have an engagement photography session in Delhi. Let us go through some of the best photo spots in Delhi for couples for your next photography session.

1. Humayun’s Tomb

One of the most popular spots for engagement photography in Delhi, the Humayun’s Tomb is best known as the precursor to the famous Taj Mahal. Taking your back to the glorious Mughal Era, this photo spot in Delhi is best for couple photography sessions that are marked with brilliant architecture and lusciously landscaped greenery.

Address: Mathura Road, Opposite Nizamuddin Dargah, New Delhi – 110013
Opening Days: 7 days open

2. Agrasen Ki Baoli

The ancient stepwell located right in the middle of Delhi is filled with photo ops for our engagement photographers in Delhi. It is a sight to behold, as it nestles quietly in the middle of all the hustle-bustle of modern streets in Central Delhi. Also considered to be one of the best spots for pre-wedding photoshoot in Delhi, this historically protected three-story stepwell with plenty of archways and a long staircase is perfect for engagement photography in Delhi.

Address: Hailey Road, KG Marg, near Diwanchand Imaging Centre, New Delhi, Delhi 110001
Opening Days: 7 days open (7:00 am – 6:00 pm)
Entry Fee: Free

3. Lodhi Colony Art District

If you consider yourself to be an artsy couple, this lesser-known and offbeat photo spot in Delhi can be the perfect destination for your Delhi pre-wedding photoshoot. Also translated as ‘India’s First Open Air, Public Art District’ this locality showcases a totally different side of Delhi to explore as the walls of the blocks are covered in vibrant murals and art installations. With each turn, you will be presented with something new for our Delhi wedding photographers to capture.

4. The Rooftop of the Old Spice Market

If you want to capture the soul of Delhi in your professional couple photoshoot, the rooftop of this famous Spice Market in Chandni Chowk- Khari Baoli can present you with incredible and sweeping views of Old Delhi as your backdrop for couple portraits. Dating back to the 17th century, it is an incredible spot to get clicked during your professional photography session in Delhi.

5. Delhi Haat Market

The enjoyable experience of Dilli Haat is not a secret to the localites of the city. Featuring handicrafts and goods from all over India, this extremely photogenic spot in Delhi is perfect for your pre-wedding photoshoot as well as wedding photography session, as the vibrant hustle and bustle of the market blur into the background like a dream.
