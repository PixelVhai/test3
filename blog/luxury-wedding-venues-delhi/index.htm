Most Popular Luxury Wedding Venues In Delhi NCR

Home » Blog » Most Popular Luxury Wedding Venues In Delhi NCR

Author: Darshana Borah

March 13, 2021 - in
  • Share
  • 39

As part of your primary wedding planning session in Delhi, choosing a wedding venue that can reverberate your magical tale will definitely need some research. Since we know that every love story is unique and if you are looking to celebrate yours, then we might have the perfect wedding venue in Delhi for you to take a step towards a new beginning.

Advertisement

While choosing a wedding venue there are so many things to take care of, whether or not it will match your requirements perfectly. Whether you want to tie the knot in a countryside wedding ceremony or a completely black tie wedding affair, or you want to go for a more traditional wedding ceremony, all your wedding visions and dreams can come true in the wide range of diverse wedding venues in Delhi.

Located at the heart of a rich cultural section of the country, these luxurious Delhi wedding venues are filled with engagement and wedding photography opportunities. Ranging from the venues soaring in the Delhi skyline to the breathtaking wedding venues in the NCR region, this list features some of the most stunning locations to host a lavish wedding affair and impress your wedding guests.

Advertisement

The exclusive and premier wedding venues in Delhi are filled with intricate architectural details, state-of-the-art urban decor, along with classic elements, furnishings, and linens that can charm anybody by its first look. These venues are famous for their world-class experience and exemplary reception. You can choose exquisite suites and guest rooms from the top hotel wedding venues in Delhi to accommodate your special guests to treat them with outstanding hospitality.

List of 9 Best Luxury Wedding Venues in Delhi NCR

  1. The Leela Palace, Chanakyapuri
  2. Essex Farms, Hauz Khas
  3. The Taj Palace, Diplomatic Enclave
  4. Calista Resort, Kapashera
  5. The Roseate, Samalka
  6. The Claridges, Connaught Place
  7. The Lalit Hotel, Barakhamba
  8. The Woods, Greenwood City
  9. ITC Grand Bharat, Gurugram

Most exclusive & luxurious wedding venues in Delhi

1. The Leela Palace, Chanakyapuri

Lying in proximity to Connaught Place and all the top-visited places of Delhi, adorning the skyline like a magnificent jewel, the Leela Palace in Chanakyapuri with its posh environs is meant for the elites.

One of the most honored and respected names when it comes to star hotels in Delhi, the royal and grand palace is an utterly luxurious wedding venue in Delhi NCR that is home to numerous private gardens, banquets, and terrace areas to tie the knot, while the magnificent city lights pose as the breathtaking backdrop for your wedding photography session in Delhi.

With a guest accommodation capacity of up to 500 people, this is one of the best Delhi wedding venues for your new beginnings.

Advertisement
Venue Address: Africa Ave, Diplomatic Enclave, Chanakyapuri, New Delhi, Delhi 110023
Contact No.: 011 3933 1234

2. Essex Farms, Hauz Khas

When we talk about luxury and grand things we cannot miss out on Essex Farms in Hauz Khas with a unique air and ambiance that can make your most special day even more special.

This posh wedding venue in Delhi is home to some of the most luxurious rooms to offer your out of town guest with special accommodation facilities and various other amenities, and the endless gourmet cuisine for your wedding menu in both veg and nonveg is a definite treat to your taste buds. Book your wedding at Essex Farms and get the best of royal treatment on your wedding day.

Venue Address: 4, Sri Aurobindo Marg, Vijay Mandal Enclave, Hauz Khas, New Delhi, Delhi 110016
Contact No.: 098990 03311

3. The Taj Palace, Diplomatic Enclave

Located in one of the poshest and prestigious Diplomatic Enclave of Delhi, the weddings at Taj Palace is truly one of the grandest and exquisite experiences for all the people with a keen eye for fine details.

This luxurious Delhi wedding venue is spread across 6 acres of lush green grounds and Mughal inspired architecture, and too expansive ballrooms known as Shahjahan and Durball Hall to host your grand opulent wedding day reception amidst breathtaking and picture risk details for your unforgettable and candid wedding photography session in Delhi. For all the intimate weddings the several banquet halls named after famous Mughal personalities along with all the exclusive outdoor wedding ceremony options make it one of the most versatile wedding venues in Delhi.

Venue Address: 2 Sardar Patel Marg, Diplomatic Enclave, New Delhi , 110021
Contact No.: 011 2611 0202

4. Calista Resort, Kapashera

Considered to be one of the most perfect and luxurious destinations to host your wedding in South Delhi, Calista Resort in Kapashera. The stunning and lavish accommodation facilities along with unparalleled service make it a perfect choice for a destination wedding in Delhi.

Advertisement

The expansive area along with plush details is located near the Delhi Airport which can be a convenient perk for your out of town guests. Ranging from air-conditioned banquet halls to courtyard weddings to outdoor lawn weddings, options to host your grand wedding ceremony and reception at this South Delhi estate can be immense and endless.

Venue Address: 37/2-4 Old, NH 48, Kapas Hera, New Delhi, Delhi 110037
Contact No.: 011 4103 4591

5. The Roseate, Samalka

A name that is synonyms to extravagance and grandeur, The Roseate in Samalka can be your dream come true destination for both intimate as well as grand wedding affair in Delhi. Surrounded by nature’s bounty and tranquility, this urban luxury resort is undeniably among one of the best wedding venues in Delhi NCR that looks like a sheer dream.

The breathtaking architecture that is also considered to be a deluxe staycation destination, makes it a 5-star wedding venue in Delhi with an accommodation capacity of up to 200 people. Famous for the poolside cocktail parties and lavish details in each corner, the ultimate hunt for the most perfect wedding venue in Delhi ends here.

Venue Address: National Highway 8, D Block, Samalka, New Delhi, Delhi 110037
Contact No.: 011 3355 2211

6. The Claridges, Connaught Place

Located centrally and known for hosting innumerable top-notch celebrity weddings and parties, The Claridges in Connaught Place is the ultimate destination for couples with a taste for blending contemporary and traditional styles when it comes to hosting the wedding day.

With three expansive and luxurious wedding reception halls that can accommodate up to 300 people, featuring pool facing spaces and an indoor wedding reception space with a grand crystal chandelier as the centerpiece, this is considered to be one of the most unique Delhi wedding venues that can host guest lists of any length and breadth.

Venue Address: 12 Dr APJ Abdul Kalam Road, New Delhi – 110011, India
Contact No.: 011 3955 5000

7. The Lalit Hotel, Barakhamba

Another five-star hotel wedding reception venue in Delhi, The Lalit is a very prominent and household name for hosting some of the most luxurious and grand weddings that the city has ever seen. The exceptionally talented culinary team has some of the most reputed badges under its name for its unmatched services in hosting wedding ceremonies and receptions.

Advertisement

One of the most luxurious and excellent wedding venues in NCR, this star wedding event space also features an exotic bar setup, in-house DJ’s and wine sommeliers. With the capacity of hosting weddings for more than 2000 people, the centrally located hotel in Delhi is a very prominent address for premier wedding celebrations.

Venue Address: Barakhamba Avenue, Connaught Place, New Delhi-110001
Contact No.: 011 4444 7777

8. The Woods, Greenwood City

For an intimate and luxurious wedding celebration, you can check out The Woods in Gurugram. This rustic and vintage themed wedding venue in the outskirts of Delhi features old countryside charm in all its glory for an unforgettable wedding day celebration. Spread over lusciously landscaped grounds, sparkling fountains, towering trees, and serene poolsides, the stunning wedding venue near Delhi combines modern amenities with traditional hospitality. Located centrally, your wedding guests can take advantage of all the luxurious facilities by checking in and enjoying a comfortable stay.

Venue Address: H- Block, Greenwood City, Sector- 46, Gurugram (Gurgaon) – 122001 India
Contact No.: 0124 492 4399

9. ITC Grand Bharat, Gurugram

One of the most stunning and palatial wedding venues for all the royal and premiere wedding affairs in Delhi NCR, ITC Grand Bharat in Gurugram is India’s first all-suites luxury retreat. It is the ultimate expression of luxury and grandeur with its exotic architecture blended with the natural glory of the ancient Aravalis. Packed with more than a hundred guest suites, four presidential villas, and signature golf courses, let your wedding guests indulge in the ultimate culinary experience and host of recreational facilities during your wedding day celebrations.

Venue Address: P.O. Hasanpur Tauru, Gurugram, Haryana 122105
Contact No.: 01267 285 500

Delhi being the hotspot for weddings in Northern India, it is filled with all kinds of wedding venues that will completely suit your style and accommodate all your big and small details to your wedding day. Ranging from the rich and exquisite traditional themes that go in with the original and local north Indian traditions to completely unique themes like western and modern wedding themes with countryside wedding decor, the culturally and traditionally diverse population of Delhi has given rise to a wide range of different wedding themes and decors to inspire your wedding.

Advertisement

If you are a local Delhiite couple who is planning to host your wedding, you might be already knowing the elegance and historical charm of these exclusive wedding venues in Delhi that has been turning dreams into reality with its sheer ambiance and amenities for perfect wedding celebrations. Find your best wedding venue in Delhi, and speak volumes for your luxurious and grand personalities in a unique way.
