The makeup also includes Nail Paint, Draping, Hair Wash and hair-styling. You will be charged extra for falsies as well as hair extensions or nail extensions.

5. Makeup Anu Kaushik

Does love copying celebrity makeup look? If you are all for moving beyond your comfort zone and trying out a different and ravishing celebrity makeup look, Anu Kaushik is the MUA you should be going to fit in with. With coloured liners, dark lips, and vibrant eye shadow, she brings out the ‘bold’ in you!

Location: Chattarpur, New Delhi

Jasmeet Kapany is a famous makeup artist in South Delhi who is very recommended and is trusted by many for a flawless, natural look! She focuses on the eyes, that tells it all and soul and it is visible in every bride of her since then. She knows how to bring out the best & shining in you!

Location- Defence Colony, New Delhi

7. Shruti Sharma Makeup

A talented and passionate artist, Shruti has created a great, bespoken space for herself among the makeup artist circle. Known for her pretty reminder lipstick and cheerful nature, she believes in bringing out the bride’s personality through her makeup techniques. She does paid trials on request. If you want to look best on your day, book them through their social media, hurry!

Location- DLF Phase 1, Gurgaon

8. Vidya Tikari

Vidya Tikari has worked her magic on countless brides over the years. Those who have hired her will vouch for her professionalism, experience, creativity and flexibility and this includes numerous brides and Bollywood heroines to name a few are Madhuri Dixit, Bipasha Basu, Malaika Arora Khan, etc. Her studio boasts many well-trained makeup artists, who are professional and well experienced.

Location – Greater Kailash I, New Delhi,

9. Sakshi Malik Studio

Her scope could be more contemporary but her style is chic and classic! With a variety of years of experience in fashion, beauty, bridal and celebrity makeup, Sakshi Malik is among the highest new-age makeup artists of Delhi. She has worked with magazines such as Marie Claire, Cosmopolitan, The Man, Femina, Maxim, Atelier on fashion and editorial shoots.

Location – DLF Pinnacle, Gurgaon

10. Ambika Pillai

Ambika Pillai is synonymous with perfection! She is an amazing bridal makeup artist in Delhi. Her portfolio is very impressive and she’s won numerous awards. She can transform you into a dreamy bride with the easiest of methods. Whether you would like to be the brilliant sparkling bride or want to look elegantly subtle at your wedding Ambika Pillai and her team bring you what you exactly want on your dream wedding occasion!

Location-  Hauz Khas Market, New Delhi,

11. Rehat Brar Bridal Makeup Artist

Known as a contemporary celebrity makeup artist, Rehat has worked with some of your favourite Bollywood brides like Sonam Kapoor, Kajol, etc. She will share all those celebrity makeup secrets with you and will give you high fashion bridal look. Her forte lies in the eye makeup which is always on point.

Location – West Delhi

12. Supriti Batra

Supriti Batra Makeup Studio – Supriti Batra is one among the top-rated makeup artists in Gurgaon. She has her salon in Gurgaon that provides all services from doing up your hair, draping your saree and a wonderful makeup look. She has been working with brides for eight years and has now become a well- known & recognized name on the marriage makeup circuit.

Location: DLF Phase 1, Gurgaon
