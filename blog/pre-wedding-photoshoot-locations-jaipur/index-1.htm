Free Pre-Wedding Shoot Locations In Jaipur

1. Jagat Shiromani Temple

Situated in the back side of Amer, Jagat Shiromani Temple is one of the most beautiful temples of Jaipur. If you believe in the tales of local folk, the temple is situated in the campus where Jodha Bai worships Krishna. It’s a worth visiting place having fabulous architecture.

Shiromani Temple is an amazing place to get a photoshoot done with the love of your life. If you want your snapshots to look divine, this is your place.

2. Patrika Gate

Jaipur is filled with pre-wedding shoot locations and Patrika gate is one of them. The infrastructure is commendable which makes it a marvelous place to capture moments with your loved one. The architectural designs are jaw-dropping which makes it an accurate place to add life to your images.

3. Hawa Mahal

You would be familiar with this name because of its beauty and charm.  It’s also known as the “palace of winds”. Hawa Mahal has extremely brilliant and beautifully carved buildings. It is a fantastic area to capture the magic in the form of shots.

4. Albert Hall Museum

If you’re in Jaipur, you just can not miss this place. It’s famous for its historical sites and magnificent framework. It’s a remarkable place for a pre-wedding photoshoot which will deliver an appeal to your pictures.

5. Amer Fort

Amer Fort is also named Amber Fort. It is located in Amer, Rajasthan. The palace is the maternal home of hindu rajput princes Jodha Bai married to a Muslim king, Akbar. Every spot is perfect. The style of construction will steal your heart.

The memorial will provide a polished and imaginative look to your shots. Add to that you can also capture several silhouette pictures in the perfect lighting.

6. Jal Mahal/Man Sagar Lake

This is your stop if you’re in search of a perfect place with a serene atmosphere. A great villa having a wonderful view situated in the middle of Man Sagar Lake. A gorgeous place to get yourself clicked and the best part is the entry is free, yes you read it right.

7. Panna Meena Kund

It’s one of the most prominent photoshoot areas. If the step-wells interest you then this enchanting Panna Meena Kund might be the perfect place for you. Many tourists come here every year to take mesmerizing pictures.

8. Birla Mandir Garden

Birla mandir garden is a historical temple that is quite famous among travelers. People from all over the world come here to click wonderful shots and enjoy the beauty of this lovely place. The structure is nothing but incredible which will be perfect for your couple shoots.

9. Maharaniyon Ki Chhatriyan

Jaipur has some amazing heritage sites, and Maharaniyon Ki Chhatriyan is one of them. Spending time over here feels surreal considering the beauty of this place. It was an extraordinary funeral area for all the regal women of Jaipur. It’s known for its incredibly beautiful cenotaphs. It has an excellent area that will be perfect for pre-wedding photoshoots. It’s a place that you wouldn’t want to miss.

10. Sambhar Salt Lake

This is an extremely stunning place which would be ideal for your Photoshoots as it will give the best background to your photos. You should go out and explore the beauty of this place to make you feel relaxed and fresh. Add to that it’s the country’s largest inland salt lake. The serenity of this place touches the soul. Sambhar Salt Lake is not like any other lake, it’s an exquisite one which you should visit.

Premium Pre-Wedding Shoot Locations In Jaipur

1. Vidhyadhar Ka Bagh

The amazing garden is situated near Sisodia Rani Ka Bagh. It’s one of the most beautiful gardens located in the city. It’s preferred by many couples for pre-wedding shoots because of its grace. Make use of effects to add interesting elements to your images.

2. Jaigarh Fort

Forts have become one of the most preferred spots for photoshoots. So get ready with your favorite outfits and visit this charming place. This place serves as an amazing backdrop for your Photoshoots which instantly helps in adding magic to your photos. Its greenery and the style of the monument are just breathtaking. You will surely get blown away by the outcomes.

3. Nahargarh Fort

Forts are truly one of the best picks when it comes to photoshoots. That might be the reason, Amir Khan picked this fort as one of the locations for his movie ‘Rang De Basanti’.

Nahargarh Fort situated in Jaipur should be on top of your list if you’re in search of a perfect location. It has a huge campus and reflects Rajputana’s architecture that you can use and make the most out of it for getting desired pictures.

4. Charan Mandir

Many people don’t know this but Charan Mandir is one of the desired places in the city. The architectural technique and the work designs are just commendable. It has stunning backgrounds that you can use to get ready to be blown away by your pictures.

5. Kanak Vrindavan Garden

Kanak Vrindavan Garden is a fantastic place that has the potential to attract thousands of visitors. It’s one of the places you wouldn’t want to miss. The serene atmosphere soothes your soul and it is one of the insta worthy places that we all love.

6. Jaipur City Palace

Do yourself a favor and visit this impressive place. Trust us, you will not regret it. The backgrounds, architecture, style of designs everything is worth applauding which makes it a one-stop destination for all your couple Photoshoots.
