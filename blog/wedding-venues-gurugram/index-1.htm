If you are planning a wedding, the first thing you need to decide is the venue. It is because of the two main reasons. First, all the good venues get booked really fast, so you might run out of the best options. Second, the venue influences a lot of other factors in your wedding, especially the decor and food. If you have not made a choice for a venue yet, this is the right time to take action.

1. Taj City Centre Gurugram

Sector 44, Near Fortis Hospital, Gurgaon, Haryana 122004

Taj City Centre offers some of the most exotic and luxurious wedding venues in the city. With a multilingual staff to take care of your guests and all your wedding ceremonies, you can simply share your requirements and relax until the day of the wedding.

You can choose any venue depending on the number of guests. Their venues have a capacity of 20 to 550 guests. From alcohol to DJ and from food to decor, they take care of everything. However, if you have any special preference, then you can hire a decorator from outside.

2. Golden Tulip Suites Gurgaon

Gurgaon-Faridabad Road, Gwal Pahadi, Sector 2, Gurugram, Haryana 122003

Formerly known as Prism Ballroom, this is a venue that looks like it has popped out of a fairytale.  The sheer magnificence of the venue will leave your guests awe-struck.

With a plush and luxurious interior that shows sheer grandeur and opulence, this is the best wedding venue if you want a royal gala as a wedding.

Moreover, every single element, be it the food counters, the table centerpieces, or even the cutlery, looks stunning and adds class to your grand event. Not to mention, their hospitality team makes your wedding a cherishable event.

You can choose the decorators from the enlisted panel and other facilities including catering, alcohol, and DJ will be provided in-house.

3. Hyatt Regency Gurgaon

New Delhi NCR NH8, Sector 83, Gurgaon, Haryana 122004

While talking about luxurious venues, Hyatt Regency Gurgaon is a name that must be included. If you are looking for a personalized wedding that suits your style, this could be your pick. No matters the type of wedding you wish to be arranged, they will ensure a perfect experience for you.

With 6 different venues to choose from, depending on the size of the event, they provide various services, including professional event planners, decorators, floral arrangements, catering, alcohol, and DJ.

With all the facilities in one place, you don’t have to worry about finding service providers for all these services. However, they also allow outside DJs.

4. Radisson Hotel Gurugram Sohna Road City Center

Radisson Hotel Gurugram Sohna Road City Center, Sohna Road, Block S, Sector 49, Gurgaon, Gurgaon, Haryana, India

With 7 different venues to choose from, Radisson (Sohna Road City Center) is all about luxurious grace and elegance. If you want a venue with an eloquent yet sophisticated ambiance, you can pick this one. By blending Indian touches with the latest international trends, they have achieved great recognition in the industry.

All the services from catering to DJ and from alcohol to decorations are provided in-house, which means you need not worry about anything. With venues for every occasion, you can book them for all your wedding-related ceremonies.

5. Park Plaza Gurgaon

B Block, Sushant Lok, Phase 1, Gurgaon, Haryana 122002

Park Plaza also presents a luxurious wedding venue for all your pre-marital and wedding ceremonies. Whether you need a rooftop wedding, a grand theme wedding, or if you wish to tie the knot alongside the turquoise blue pool, they have a venue to suit all your requirements.

There is no limit to the creativity and imagination for the decorations. In fact, here your decorations could be perfectly in sync with your dream wedding. You can choose any decorator from the enlisted panel for an enchanting and dreamy decoration.

Park Plaza offers 4 different venues along with their high-class services including catering, decor, DJ, and alcohol. The services are either in-house or you can make a choice from the enlisted panel.

6. South Patio Club, Gurgaon

South Patio Club, South City II, Sector 50, Gurugram, Haryana, India

If you have a fetish for poolside wedding celebration amidst luxury, we will highly recommend South Patio Club. With a beautiful pool and ample space, you can have a delightful and totally dreamy theme wedding.

In fact, bridal entrances on a boat or the ‘Jaimal’ ceremony over the pool are quite prominent these days. If you are one of those people who wish to make a statement at your wedding or want the bride to make an entrance on a boat, this would be a perfect venue.

The pool, here, is quite large and you can imagine the lovely pictures and videos of such an event. Moreover, all the services are provided in-house to save you the trouble of looking for several vendors.

7. The Pllazio Hotel, Gurgaon

292-296, Sector 29, City Center, Gurgaon, Haryana 122001

The Pllazio Hotel is specifically known for its charismatic ambiance and decor. Not to mention, the best-in-class services, delectable cuisines, and the fact that you get all the services under one roof.

No matters what your preferences are, they have the perfect solution for you. Be it the colors, the flowers, or the theme of your wedding, they can surely exceed your expectations. Moreover, they take care of the tiniest of details to give you an enigmatic and memorable wedding.

8. Unitech Club Patio, Gurgaon

Unitech Club Patio, Block E, South City I, Sector 41, Gurugram

We have yet another poolside wedding venue option for you. With a total of 5 wedding venues to choose from, Unitech Club Patio could be a perfect option for you.

Let us give you another reason. If you wish to perform the ‘Jaimala’ ritual surrounded by water on all sides with waves reflecting light over you, it will be hard to find a better place than this.

Here, it would be a rather relaxing and enjoyable event for you because all the services like catering, DJ, alcohol, and decor are provided in-house.

9. Ramada Gurgaon Central

Site No 2, Sector 44, Gurgaon, Haryana 122003

While discussing the luxurious wedding venues in Gurgaon, Ramada Gurgaon Central is a name we just can’t overlook. The precision and attention to detail is their expertise and that is what makes your events mesmerizing and enrapturing.

With an ambiance that looks classy and magnificent at the same time, you can arrange the wedding of your choice. Moreover, the opulence and grandeur of the venue allow for a lot of experiment with the decor for added charm.

You can choose the decorators from the enlisted panel and the rest of the services are provided in-house.

10. Galaxy Hotel & Spa, Gurugram

NH – 8, Sector – 15, Part – II, Beside 32nd Milestone Hotel, Gurgaon

Galaxy Hotel is yet another option for a luxurious wedding venue. Your guests will be enthralled with the opulence, decor, and charms of your wedding venue. Moreover, there is not a speck of a doubt that your wedding photography and videography will be amazing. Not to mention, your wedding will be a memorable event in this luxurious retreat.

With 5 different venue options and all in-house services except for the decorators, that you may choose from the enlisted panel, this makes a very strong contender for your wedding venue.

11. Crowne Plaza Gurgaon

Crowne Plaza Near Traffic Park, Gurgaon, Haryana, India

With 5 venue options, out of which two are outdoor, two indoor, and one poolside, this makes a perfect wedding venue that caters to all types of gatherings for your wedding. They also offer all the services in-house and you can choose the decorators from their trusted panel.

If you need a royal residential wedding to show your true personality and taste, Crowne Plaza is an option that you must consider.

12. Clarens Hotel – Leisure Valley, Gurgaon

Plot No. 363-364, Sector 29, Gurgaon, Haryana 122001

If you love the color white and want pristine white marbles in your venue, you must consider Clarens Hotel. It is a luxury boutique hotel situated in sector 29, a prime location in Gurgaon which is easily accessible.

They offer all the services you need for your wedding and they have a panel from where you can choose your decorators. With 3 venues to choose from and a wide variety of international cuisines in their menu, you can make a statement through your wedding.

13. Hotel Zone By The Park, Gurugram Central

Gurugram Central, Sector 29, Gurugram, Haryana

An easy to locate and beautiful venue in the most happening areas of Gurugram is what you need for your wedding and Zone is a luscious venue that fills these requirements rather easily. It is spacious and equipped with leading facilities.

To cut things short, this could be the place where you can organize your dream wedding because there is a lot of scope for decorative grandeur and beauty. They offer all the services from decor to catering and from alcohol to DJ.

14. Royal Swan Banquet, Gurgaon

Royal swan banquet, Sector 33, Gurugram, Haryana, India

If you are considering banquets more than the hotels, this is one of the best choices that you have in Gurugram. Their decor and their premises are what sets them apart. Be their interiors or exteriors, everything looks like a beautiful picture painted on a grand canvas.

The aura they create is enchanting and it makes way for a wedding event that is hard to forget. The best part about them is they offer the decor services themselves and this is one of the main reasons for their success.

All their services are in-house except catering. Outside vendors for catering are allowed.

15. Emaar Club Beryl, Gurugram

Golf Course Ext Road, Sector 65, Gurugram, Haryana 122018

Club Beryl is situated on the outskirts of Gurugram and this makes it a highly desirable location. If you wish to host your wedding celebrations away from the constant hustle and noise of the city, this could be a perfect venue for you to escape the constant blaring of honks.

Not only do they offer luxurious comfort and gorgeous decorations, but you also get a lot of scenic beauty all around, making it a perfect venue for a nature lover. Now, if we talk about the services, they permit other vendors for decor purposes and they provide the rest.

16. Club florence, Gurugram

Club Florence, Block E, Sushant Lok 2, Sector 56, Gurugram, Haryana, India

Talking of nature enthusiasts, Club Florence is also a great wedding venue that focuses on sustainable efforts. Around 75% of their total property is covered in greenery which makes it a suitable venue for nature lovers. However, this is not the only reason to select this venue.

The enigmatic and creative decorations and the flexibility to incorporate any decor is something that makes this location much more desirable. You can hire a decorator from their trusted panel and all other services are provided in-house.
