Best Places To Go For A Winter Honeymoon in India

The following list provides the ultimate information you need regarding your perfect romantic holiday. These are the top 10 best honeymoon places that you can visit in India. Choose any of the places that fall under your budget and taste.

1. Dalhousie, Himachal Pradesh

Dalhousie is a place that is preferred by many people for their honeymoon period. This place has a tremendous amount of facilities that will you will cherish. It’s one of the most popular hill stations that will be perfect for your honeymoon.

It’s a remarkable place to visit if you’re looking for holiday options. The weather is great that will make you fall in love with each other over and over again. There are several adventurous activities that you can do to make the most out of. Also, you can get a sneak peek and get to know more about their culture that is practiced there.

2. Coorg (Kodagu), Karnataka

When it comes to making your life Magical, Coorg has a lot to offer. It’s perfect for couples as it has some of the best adventures ever. It’s popular for its exquisite destinations. The beauty of the place often attracts thousands of visitors and helps them create lifelong memories.

You will not believe but Coorg is also known as the “Scotland of India.” Which makes it even more special.

3. Nainital, Uttarakhand

Get ready to celebrate love with a little bit of coziness by visiting this gorgeous place. The weather is so romantic that you will love it. It’s an excellent place having breathtaking scenery. Nainital is loved by people from all over the world and every year thousands of visitors come to witness the beauty of this place.

It’s surrounded by mountains which will add essence to your life. It has beautiful lakes where you can feel peace.

4. Jaisalmer, Rajasthan

Jaisalmer (The Golden city) is famous for a lot of things that include forts, lakes, dunes, cuisine, etc.
Couples are often in search of a place that is adventurous and calming at the same time.

They like living in the moment as the honeymoon is a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity that you wouldn’t want to miss. Jaisalmer is the right place to make you experience memorable things and events. It’s a place that has elegant backgrounds that will enhance the beauty of the pictures.

Read Also: Royal Pre Wedding Shoot Location in Jaipur Rajasthan

5. Udaipur, Rajasthan

Udaipur (City of Lakes) is famous for its culture and heritage. It has many attractions that will win our hearts. It’s perfect to celebrate love in this gorgeous place. If the serene atmosphere interests you, Udaipur is where you need to go.

It’s filled with fun and adventure that you will adore all your life. It doesn’t have chaos like that of the city.

6. Goa

When talking about honeymoon and vacations, How can we forget Goa? Goa (Pearl of Orient) is one of the most preferred places that is loved by millions of people from all over the world. The beaches, serenity, fun, are just commendable.

It has a lot of significant places that you should travel to spend a good time. There is an endless number of romantic activities that you can do in Goa so don’t forget to check it out.

7. Shimla, Himachal Pradesh

Believe it or not, hill stations are the purest form of love. They are filled with fun and joy. They bring so much happiness to everyone and offer a lot of amenities.

Shimla has admirable stays that make the process even easier. The capital city of Himachal Pradesh is an ideal place to spend your honeymoon and create unforgettable memories.

8. Manali, Himachal Pradesh – Popular Honeymoon Destination for Winter

You probably would have seen Manali in almost every trekking movie. It’s a beautiful place to visit which offers the best hostility and arrangements.

You don’t need to go to any other place if you can to Manali. It’s adorned by many people which we believe is obvious as it’s nothing but perfect for holidays.

9. Darjeeling, West Bengal

Darjeeling is one of the places that should be on your bucket list. Its stunning range of mountains and the beauty of nature makes people attract to it. It offers loads of activities that will provide you an unforgettable experience. Couples are welcome anytime they wish to visit.

10. Auli, Uttarakhand

Uttarakhand has several places that make us fall in love with nature. Well, Auli is one of them. It offers a lot of fun stuff that people love. Make sure to check out this place if you’re an enthusiast of hill stations. It offers one of the best romantic experiences that you will love.

11. Kashmir

If there’s one place that represents the beauty of nature, it’s Kashmir. Famously known as “heaven on the earth”, Kashmir never fails to disappoint travelers and even couples. Its mesmerizing beauty captures the heart and makes everyone fall in love.

If you’re in search of a cozy honeymoon trip then Kashmir should be on the top of your list. Why? Because it has everything you can ever ask for. From breathtaking sight scenes to delicious food, it has it all. It would be a perfect destination for love birds as it has numerous fun activities.

To name a few, trekking mountains, skiing, mountain biking, fishing, rafting, boating, snowboarding, etc. are some of the popular ones. It has awesome sight scenes that are perfect for a honeymoon couple.

There are several places in Kashmir that you can make the most out of and have the best time ever. Water houseboats are available here which makes everything even more delightful and wonderful. Places like Gulmarg, Pahalgam, Srinagar are some of the finest places for a vacation in Kashmir. So pack your bags and get ready to travel to an enchanting place to have an awesome time.

12. Andaman and Nicobar islands

Andaman and Nicobar islands is a dreamy place that will blow your mind with its exquisite beauty. It has enchanting beaches, oceans, islands, parks and so many other interesting things. It’s an ideal location that makes an ideal destination for a honeymoon travel vacation.

Andaman is an amazing place to spend some quality time with your loved one as it has a lesser population compared to the other states. The impressive scenic views, breathtaking beaches will add wonders to your over-all trip and make it unforgettable. It’s one of the best places to travel during your special time to create thousands of memories.

Add to that it has various fun activities like snow, surfing, scuba diving, etc. It also has stunning resorts for wonderful accommodation that you should check out.

13. Ooty, Tamil Nadu

What would be more special than visiting Ooty for your honeymoon? Well, Ooty has some of the best romantic places that will make your vacation a lot more memorable than usual. It has beautiful locations that will cheer up your mood and make you feel alive.

From amazing food to excellent accommodation, Ooty has a lot to offer. It is one of the best places for a honeymoon vacation. It offers a wide range of amenities and services to make your journey easy and safe. People from all over the world come and visit this gorgeous place.

Best Honeymoon Destinations outside India in December

Wanting an execusite experience out of the country on your honeymoon? Not to worry! We have a special list ready. Just check them below.

1. Mauritius

Mauritius is an excellent place to travel with your partner and create loads of memories. People from all over the world come and visit this place as it’s extremely gorgeous.

The view that it serves is just another level. Its beauty can’t be expressed in words but it’s worth visiting. Trust us, you will fall in love with this place.

2. Bali, Indonesian Island

It’s one of the most loved honeymoon places ever. Bali is very famous for its adventure sports and romantic weather. It’s extremely tough to leave this place as it holds a place in the hearts. Bali is a place that is on every traveler’s list.

The islands are so beautiful that you will end up taking tons of pictures of them.

3. Paris, City of France

Everyone has dreamt of visiting Paris once in their lifetime. Why? Because it’s like a hub of fun, adventure, fashion, romance, etc. The Eiffel tower is one of the most famous places all around the world. People also call it the “city of love” which is incredible. There are plenty of things to do in Paris.

4. The Maldives

The Maldives is a happy place that is filled with joy. It offers a wide range of activities that you can do. Its beautiful beaches attract a lot of couples.

You can book a villa and live your dream life come true within this magical place. It has a bunch of attractions that you will all love. Make your bond special by visiting the Maldives. Book Maldives Honeymoon Packages for Couple

5. Utah, United States

If skiing interests you then this is your place. Utah is situated in the United States and is a wonderful place to visit on your honeymoon. Also, It has one of the best resorts that you should not miss. It’s a wonderful place for a romantic honeymoon.

6. Hawaii, US States

You all have heard or read about Hawaii in your childhood. It’s famous for its islands which are generally known as “Hawaiian Islands.”

It’s a beautiful escape from your day to lives and would be a perfect place to spend your best days with your loved one. It’s a place that captures the heart.

7. Caribbean, The Caribbean Sea

The Caribbean is one of the most exotic honeymoon destinations. Its islands are perfect for a romantic stay. This place will give you a sense of relief and joy that will always cherish. Everything about the Caribbean is worth enjoying.

8. New Zealand

New Zealand is an exquisite place for honeymoons. It’s surrounded by islands, mountains, and so many other stunning places.

New Zealand is the epitome of beauty which you should check out. The magnificent islands contribute to enhancing the beauty of the place.

9. Swiss Alps, Central European country

Swiss Alps is the perfect place if you love snowboarding, skiing, etc. It’s a breathtaking place with gorgeous scenery. People mostly prefer doing adventurous sports and activities here.

Enjoy your honeymoon in this exotic destination. It’s one of the ultimate places to go on the honeymoon this December.

10. Greece

Whenever thinking about holidays, Greece never disappoints. It’s like a no-brainer when it comes to vacations and honeymoons.

It’s a place that will add positive energy to your married life. It’s famous for its delicious food, worth praising spots and so many other things. It’s a stunning place that should be definitely on your list.

Wrapping Up

The whole world gets covered from cold waves in the month of December. Despite the cold, winter grants us the best views and places to build memories. Find the best winter honeymoon destination today and book in advance for best deals and to avoid last minute rush.
