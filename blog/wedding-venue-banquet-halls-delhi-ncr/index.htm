10 Best Venue & Banquet Halls Suggestions For Delhi NCR Wedding 

Home » Blog » 10 Best Venue & Banquet Halls Suggestions For Delhi NCR Wedding 

Author: Alka Singh

February 8, 2021 - in
  • Share
  • 39

Booking the best venue at the best location comes as a first priority when a wedding date gets decided. The venue of a wedding adds vibrancy and creates the perfect ambiance for the occasion and guests.

Advertisement

If you are going to wed in Delhi NCR, then you don’t need to fret since several best venues and banquet halls are available to accommodate all sorts of needs. The properties are well-designed and built to provide an unforgettable experience to you and your guests. But, here comes the confusion of how to find the best Banquet Halls in Delhi NCR among many. To help you make better and faster decisions, here we are presenting our suggestion of 10 best venues and banquet halls:

List of 10 Best Wedding Venues & Banquet Halls in Delhi NCR

  1. The Ritz by Ferns N Petals Gardens – Luxury Banquet Hall Gurugram
  2. Atrio Hotel, A Boutique Hotel  – Wedding Venue in Kapashera, Delhi
  3. Golden Tulip, Chattarpur – Banquet Hall in Chhatarpur, Delhi
  4. ITC WelcomHotel – 5 Star Wedding Venue Dwarka, Delhi
  5. Amarai Farms, Kapashera – Wedding Banquet Halls in Delhi
  6. Country Inn & Suites By Radisson, Sahibabad (Ghaziabad) – Wedding Hotel in East Delhi NCR
  7. The Lalit, Connaught Place – 5 Star Wedding Hotel Delhi
  8. Crowne Plaza – 5 Star Wedding Hotels Gurgaon
  9. The Westin,  Gurgaon – Luxury Wedding Hotel Delhi-NCR
  10. The Golden Treat, Sahibabad, Delhi

Most Popular Delhi NCR BASED Luxury Banquet Hall & 5 Star Venues for Wedding, Engagement and Reception Ceremony

1. The Ritz by Ferns N Petals Gardens

The Ritz by Ferns N Petals Gardens – Luxury Banquet Hall Gurugram

Advertisement

If you are looking for a luxurious venue or are planning for a signature wedding, The Ritz by Ferns N Petals Gardens should be your choice. It is a closed banquet property and it is situated at the prime location of Ambience Island, Gurgaon. This boutique is stunning with its amazing decorations making it the best place that adds luxury to your wedding function.

It is massive and spacious and occupies a large number of gatherings making it the best boutique in the city. If you want your wedding to take place in luxury and decorations, Ritz boutique hall in NCR is the one you’re looking for.

SERVICES & FACILITIES

  • Area – 60,000 sq. ft
  • Capacity – 1500 – 3000 guests
  • Parking Capacity – 2000 cars

2. Atrio – A Boutique Hotel, Kapashera, Delhi

This hotel is a perfect combination of modern and contemporary. The boutique hall is stunning with its aesthetic interior. The decorations, lighting, and arrangements made in this hotel will leave you amazed.

Atrio has earned a great popularity as a wedding venue given its large space to facilitate both indoor and outdoor settings and guest satisfaction. This luxurious property has 3 banquet halls namely Venezia, Victoria and Veneto built to accommodate 800, 1400 and 150 guests respectively.

Advertisement

It also has rooms built & quipped with ample amenities including free Wi-Fi and king size beds. Atrio is the perfect destination to make a wedding memorable and comfortable at the same time.

3. Golden Tulip, Chattarpur, Delhi

Hotel Golden Tulip Chattarpur helps add luxury and comfort to a wedding. Situated in south of Delhi with close proximity to Chattarpur metro station and Chattarpur Temple, this lavish property delivers retreat amid the chaos of Delhi NCR.

Spread over three acres, the property reflects a perfect combination of art, design and advance infrastructure with lush green lawns, beautiful rooms and suites overlooking the pool and garden, salon, state of the art conference wedding halls, spacious lawns and multicuisine restaurants serving delicacies to choose from in both vegetarian and non-vegetarian food.

4. ITC WelcomeHotel, Dwarka, Delhi

ITC WelcomHotel – 5 star Wedding Venue Dwarka, Delhi

The boutique hall in ITC Welcome is beautifully designed and decorated. The world-class facility of this hall and the best service provided by the staff here can make your wedding simple and stress-free. They take care of all arrangements and other stuff so that you and your family will be free to enjoy the wedding.

Advertisement

Though this boutique hall does not occupy a large gathering, it can be the best suit for small wedding functions. Their tasty food will leave your wedding guests jaw dropping and their services too are up to the mark.

5. Amarai Farms, Kapashera, Delhi

Amarai Farms, Kapashera – Wedding Banquet Halls in Delhi

Amarai Farms is one of the best boutique halls for all kinds of wedding functions, regardless of whether it is big or small. The prime goal of this hotel is to provide a customised service for your wedding.

They make sure that you, your family, and your guests are having a good time at your wedding. If you are a nature lover who’s looking for the perfect outdoor venue to celebrate your wedding, you must go for Amarai Farms.

6. Country Inn & Suites By Radisson, Sahibabad, East Delhi NCR

Country Inn & Suites By Radisson, Sahibabad (Ghaziabad) – Wedding Hotel in East Delhi NCR

Country Inn at Sahibabad is an amazing resort with 7 big bouquet halls out of which Darbar party hall is the famous one which has an area of 20,000 sq. ft. It can accommodate more than 1000 guests making it the best option to go with when it comes to big wedding functions.

Advertisement

You can also buy the other rooms to make it more spacious and convenient for your wedding guests to enjoy. If you are a person with a large number of family guests, this is the ideal boutique hall for you. Choose Country Inn and enjoy the luxury and comfort.

7.The Lalit, Connaught Place, Delhi

The Lalit, Connaught Place – 5 Star Wedding Hotel in Delhi

The Lalit banquet hall needs no introduction for people living in Delhi. It is one of the most famous wedding venues in Delhi that has celebrated countless weddings. If you have already been to The Lalit, it is no wonder.

It has three bouquet halls – Regency, Regal and Crystal Ballroom which can be the ideal fit to carry out weddings that have medium-sized gatherings. The dining in this 5-star hotel is world-class. They provide the best services for any hosting you plan to have.

Advertisement

8. Crowne Plaza, Gurgaon, Delhi

Crowne Plaza – 5 Star Wedding Hotels Gurgaon, DELHI NCR

Crowne Plaza hotel is present in the thriving business district of Delhi, that is Gurgaon. It is also close to the airport and other tourist places making it the best and attractive bouquet hall for grand weddings. The interior here is stunning and creative. The beauty of this place always wins its place among the top 10.

If you want to celebrate your wedding with luxury and beauty like none other, you must go to Crowne Plaza in Gurgaon. Make your wedding the most memorable one in this amazing spot.

9. The Westin – Gurgaon, Delhi

The Westin, Gurgaon – Luxury Wedding Hotel Delhi-NCR

The Westin hotel offers the best boutique hall and great services at a price that you never imagined. The ballroom of this hotel is stunning and eye-catchy. Their affordability and beauty make it stand out from the other boutique halls in the city. Celebrate your wedding here to enjoy luxury at a low cost.

10. The Golden Treat, Sahibabad, Delhi

This hotel offers lavish services for a large gathering. The interiors are also amazing and attractive. They provide good services on time making it easy for you to enjoy the function. The food here is also up to the mark. If you want to host your wedding functions with a large gathering, The Golden Treat in Sahibabad is the best option. Enjoy your wedding in lavish venues like The Golden Treat to make more memories.

Weddings are more emotional apart from all the enjoyment. Two souls are getting united along with the soles of their family. Moments like these are special and must be captured and saved. To do that, you need a perfect sport that adds extra beauty to your already existing ones. Plan your wedding in one of these venues to create an everlasting memory.

Are you still waiting? Go reserve your boutique hall right away before someone else does that. Do not compromise on your wedding venue as it plays a vital role in bringing your dream to life.
