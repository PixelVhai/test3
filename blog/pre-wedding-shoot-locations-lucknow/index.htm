Top 13 Historical Pre-Wedding Shoot Locations in Lucknow

  • Share
  • 39

Whether it’s a cocktail party just before the wedding or a pre-wedding photoshoot, you don’t want to miss a chance to spend time with bae right? Well, the wedding season is all about love and togetherness. It brings two families together. Not only it’s one of the most sacred occasions but also the beginning of married life.

Advertisement

A pre-wedding shoot is one of the most special and fun times in a couple’s life. It gives immense satisfaction to the couple by strengthening their everlasting bond. It is not just about photos, pre-wedding photoshoots let you be more acquainted with your photographer and give you another chance to spend some quality time with your beloved.

Believe it or not, pre-wedding shoots are a package full of excitement and love. They also help in making camera-shy people more comfortable for the main wedding day. Through pre-wedding shoots, you can know your photographer’s photography style and so many other things.

India being home to several beautiful states has some of the most stunning pre-wedding locations ever. ” The city of Nawabs ” is one of them. Lucknow is undoubtedly one of the most incredible places to shoot mesmerizing pictures. It’s a one-stop destination for all your wedding festivities.

Want to know some exotic pre-wedding shoot locations in Lucknow? We have got you covered. Here are some of the top 13 photoshoot locations for pre-wedding shoots in Lucknow.

Here’s Best places for Pre wedding photoshoot in Lucknow

1. British Residency (The Residency, Lucknow)

Be it winters or summers, British Residency never disappoints. Its history wins hearts and is considered one of the most incredible places to get your pre-wedding shoot done. Its beauty is magnificent and will allow you to click some gorgeous pictures. It will provide you with a magnificent background for your photos. If you are fond of nature and historical monuments then this is the place for you.

2. Bara Imambara

When coming to Lucknow, Bara Imambara is a must. One can never witness the beauty this place holds without visiting it. It is a great location to click some extraordinary pictures for your pre-wedding photoshoot. The location also has baoli and bhool – bhulaiya which makes it one of the most perfect destinations to get clicked within the historical ambiance. The gorgeous gateways, pillars, the tomb will make a perfect background for your snapshots. Honestly, nothing beats the beauty of Bara Imambara.

3. Janeshwar Mishra Park, Lucknow

Situated in Gomti Nagar, Janeshwar Mishra Park is one of the most visited places in Lucknow. Couples from all over the country come here to click some mesmerizing pictures among the beauty of nature. The lakes, gardens, pathways make it an alluring location. You can even recreate famous Bollywood scenes in this place to make your photos exciting and fun.

4. Kudia Ghat, Lucknow

Want to add a Nawabi touch to your photos? Kudia Ghat should be your go-to place. Its fascinating architecture will steal your hearts for sure. It’s wonderfully situated near the gomti river to give you the best captures. Go during the time of sunset and sunrise to make the most out of your trip. You can also indulge yourself and your bae in various fun activities such as boating to take some time off.

5. Lohia Park (Lucknow)

You may have visited many parks but nothing compares to the beauty of Lohia park ( also known as Ram Manohar Lohia Park). It is one of the romantic places to capture some mesmerizing pictures. It is a splendid location with so much serenity. This place will definitely make your photos majestic. If you’re looking for a location that doesn’t have so much crowd then you should consider visiting this place. In Lohia park, you can easily get your photoshoot done without any disturbance.

6. Sikandar Bagh Lucknow

Sikandar Bagh is a place of love. It is truly a great location for pre-wedding photoshoots. If you want to capture your emotions in photos then Sikandar bagh is one of the best places to do this. If you want to experiment with your photoshoot then try clicking photos from different angles and backgrounds with the use of props. Its historical architecture and garden make a great combination for shooting fascinating pre-wedding photos.

7. Dilkusha Garden (Kothi), Lucknow

Dilkusha Garden is yet another gorgeous place in Lucknow to click some splendid pictures with bae. This place hardly needs any introduction. The historical Kothi will provide a stunning background to your photos. The peacefulness of this place will let you click amazing pictures with no disturbance. You can also use some props to add a fun element to your photos. The lush green lawns depict the true beauty of nature. If greenery and serenity attract you then this place is ideal for you to shoot the best pictures.

8. Ambedkar Memorial Park, Lucknow

Ambedkar Park is a well-known location as it’s been featured in many songs and movies for its extraordinary beauty. This place adds an amazing natural touch to all your photos. It is spacious and has backgrounds that add a beautiful backdrop to your photos. It has numerous sightseeing elements that will do wonders for your Photoshoot. If you are in Lucknow you just can not miss this place.

9. Satkhanda, Lucknow

Situated near Husainabad Trust Road, Satkhanda is one of the most iconic architectures. It’s a four-story structure with incredible detailing. Its scenic beauty is worth praising and will make the perfect choice to shoot your pre-wedding images. Not only in the morning but this place looks extremely gorgeous at night as well. Its lighting and decorations will help you add more beauty and grace to your pre-wedding shoot. Trust us you won’t get enough of your pre-wedding in Lucknow.

10. Hussainabad Clock Tower Lucknow

Does the idea of hosting a pre-wedding shoot in an iconic clocktower excite you? Then Hussainabad Clock Tower should definitely be on your list. This is yet another heritage of Lucknow that was built in 1881. This clock tower is an awesome spot to grace the shoot and capture countless memories. Try new poses from different angles to make the most out of this place. You can also use some fun props to add candidness to the overall shoot. The glorious clock tower adds an instant charm to all of your photos.

11. Chattar Manzil (Lucknow)

Also known as an umbrella palace, Chattar Manzil is a perfect location to create some memories with your beloved. If you’re doing a pre-wedding shoot in Lucknow, this place is a must-visit. It is extremely gorgeous and has wonderful backgrounds that will make the best backdrop for your photos. It is loved by tourists from all over the world due to its mesmerizing beauty. It makes every shoot picture perfect and appealing. Whenever in Lucknow, this is a must photo stop.

12. Gomti Riverfront Park (Lucknow)

Want to live those fairytale moments with your fiance? Then you should surely opt for Gomti Riverfront. You would have heard of this place due to its breathtaking views and its aesthetically pleasing waterfront never disappoints. From boating amenities to beautiful fountains, the Gomti riverfront has it all. Not only it’s extremely beautiful but also has some of the best backgrounds that will add a glorious touch to your photos. Look no further as the Gomti riverfront is one of the best places in Lucknow to get your photoshoot done.

13. Rumi Darwaza Lucknow

Located near hussainabad road, Rumi Darwaza is yet another place to shoot some exciting pre-wedding photoshoots. It’s inspired by a famous gateway of Istanbul. It’s one of the most famous landmarks in Lucknow. It is spacious and well maintained by the authorities. Rumi Darwaza is one of the most excellent places to get captured and is the main attraction for tourists from all over the world. The beauty of this place will not only add grace to your wedding album but also make your pictures speak for themselves.

Conclusion

Pre-wedding shoots are one of the best ways to capture some valuable and intimate moments with your beloved. To save you from the hustle, we hope our exclusive list of pre-wedding shoot locations in Lucknow would be a great help. Lucknow is gifted with some of the most romantic spots which makes them photoshoot friendly. It is one of the most beautiful places with rich culture. So choose your best outfits and get ready to ace the wedding album.
