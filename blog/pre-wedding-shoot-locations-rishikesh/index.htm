22+ Free & Paid Pre wedding Shoot Locations & Resorts in Rishikesh

Ever dreamt of living a fairytale while clicking snapshots? Pre-wedding shoots are one of the best possible ways to do it. Pre-wedding shoots are extraordinary and bring so many remembrances. It’s one of the cherishing parts of marriage which holds a lot of value in people’s hearts as it captures unforgettable memories. Pictures speak for themselves, they bring endless memories that are worth praising. Well, When it comes to making love with your beloved, mountains are mostly preferred. Uttarakhand has one of the amazing Pre-wedding destinations that you will love. Rishikesh is a well-known region of Uttarakhand that has some of the best pre-wedding shoot locations.

1. Lakshman jhula and Ram jhula

Lakshman jhula and Ram jhula are known for their history and culture. People from all over the country and world come and visit this place. They hold a lot of value for thousands of people as these are connected with Lord Rama and Lakshman. One of the most amazing things is that the entry is free, which is insane as it’s a delightful place with stunning backgrounds. It will add light to your pictures, so make sure to visit the place. Both these bridges will give you phenomenal spots for your pictures.

2. The Beatles Ashram

It’s a popular ashram that is situated in Rishikesh. It’s a fantastic place that has a peaceful environment that will help you click more pictures than usual. The view is great and it serves as an awesome exotic location that you must visit. It’s termed “the Beatles” due to the hype they got after the Beatles visited this place.

3. Shivpuri, Rishikesh

Shivpuri is yet another beautiful place that you can visit for your pre-wedding shoot. It’s quite prominent for its river rafting. It’s loved by tourists who love adventures. The beauty of the river will add life to your pictures which will blow your mind. It’s also very peaceful that will let you click pictures more romantically. Visit this place to get some special time with your loved one.

4. Triveni Ghat

It’s the biggest ghat located in Rishikesh. It’s quite famous that you would have heard about this place already. It’s an incredible place to indulge yourself in several activities to have fun. Photoshoots are quite common here as it’s one of the most famous places of Rishikesh.

5. Neer Garh Waterfall, Rishikesh

Want to make your pictures magical? Go visit this place. Trust us, we won’t let you down. Neer Garh Waterfall is one of the glorious places ever. It adds a sense of grace to your pictures. It’s not like a regular waterfall, it’s much more than that. You can see the magnificence of nature here that will make you even happier. It’s ideal for photoshoots and picnics. It’s like a hidden treasure of Rishikesh that you should not miss.

6. Vashishta Gufa (Rishikesh)

It’s one of the stunning locations that is perfect for an ideal pre-wedding photoshoot. It’s so serene and peaceful. Be prepared to have an incredible photoshoot whenever coming to this place. Visitors from all over the world completely fall in love with the place. It’s like a hub of beauty so definitely visit Vashishta Gufa if you’re in search of a perfect location to click some amazing photos.

7. Muni ki Reti, Rishikesh

Muni ki Reti is a small area or town situated near Rishikesh. It has an enormous amount of temples, yoga centers, ashrams, etc.
If culture interests you, then this is your place. It’s a perfect place for those on the lookout for peace and serenity. It will make your pre-wedding photos a lot more beautiful than usual.

8. Goa Beach in Rishikesh

Who doesn’t love beaches? They are extremely gorgeous and fun to visit. They also add a touch of spice to your pictures which is rare in many shoot locations. It’s an awesome place to relax and have fun. The place is so pretty that will add an amazing backdrop to all your pictures. It’s an exotic location to capture your best moments with the love of your life.

9. Neem Beach Rishikesh

It’s yet another amazing beach that is loved by hundreds of people. It’s filled with so many adventure activities which also include river rafting. The serenity and soothing aura of this place win several hearts. Photographers also find this place amusing as it gives the best background for pre-wedding photoshoots.

10. Rishikund

Rishikund is a sacred pond situated in Rishikesh. It’s famous for its history and culture. It’s such a soothing and serene place that we all love. It has some of the best spots that you can take advantage of. So what are you waiting for? Pack your luggage and travel your heart out to click some marvelous pictures in this place.

11. Neer Gaddu Waterfall

Neer Gaddu Waterfall is a striking waterfall that is situated in Rishikesh. People often indulge themselves in adventurous activities like trekking to the waterfall to have fun. Couples on the other hand love to shoot their pre-wedding photos and enjoy moments with one another here. Basic foods like drinks, noodles are available in the shops near the waterfall.

12. Jumpin Heights (Rishikesh)

Jumpin Heights is one of the worth visiting places in Rishikesh. It’s a fun, adventurous place that will you happy. It’s always on every traveler bucket list as it’s a great place to shoot pictures, spend time together, etc.
It will surely nurture and make your bond a lot stronger.

13. Kaudiyala

Kaudiyala is a famous area that is loved by several tourists. It has river rafting and camping facilities also, which is great. It offers a wide range of other fun activities as well. Add to that, It’s a highly adventurous place that has a lot to offer.

1. Salvus Hotel Rishikesh

Whenever visiting Rishikesh never forget salvus hotel. It is known to make your trips memorable. Unlike other hotels, it offers tremendous adventure activities like hiking and rafting. It also has various spots that will capture your eyes.

2. Avenue 18 Resort

Avenue 18 Resort is a great combo of nature and luxury. It serves the best food and accommodation. Couples fall in love with the place as it has the best backdrops for pre-wedding pictures. It gives an unbelievable experience so don’t miss it out.

3. Hotel Natraj (Rishikesh)

Nevertheless, this place requires no introduction. This 4-star hotel is here to provide you the best experience ever. From free parking to yoga hall, they have it all. So do yourself a favor and visit this gorgeous place.

4. Chanakya Resort (Shivpuri, Rishikesh)

Chanakya resort is just the right place if you’re looking for a perfect romantic destination with your partner. Couples can indulge themselves in a bunch of courageous activities like River Rafting, Body Surfing, Rock Climbing, etc. It’s an ideal place that will fit your pre-wedding shoot requirements.

5. Ganga Kinare – Hotel Resort In Rishikesh

Ganga kinare is a stunning boutique offering delicious food, facilities, and accommodation. It’s one of the favorite destinations of tourists because of its awe-inspiring views. The infrastructure is amazing that will bless your eyes and would make you never want to leave.

6. Skyard Rishikesh – Hostel in Rishikesh

Love birds, this is your chance to make your stay full of romance and fun. Skyward hotel is perfect for your stay as well as pre-wedding shoots and will help you in spending quality time together. Don’t hesitate in travelling here, you will love it.

7. Soul Haven – Hostel In Rishikesh

As the name suggests, it’s pure heaven. Soul haven offers romantic-style accommodation for couples that’s what makes it one of their favorite places. Tourists love this place as the vibes of this place are just another level. Enjoy this place for a memorable photo shoot.

8. Hill Hoppers Hotel, Rishikesh

Hill Hoppers is a hotel that is widely known for its commendable services. The architectural walls add an amazing backdrop to the pictures. The beauty of this place attracts many visitors. It also offers a variety of options to choose from which is great from the consumer’s point of view.

9. Aloha On The Ganges, Rishikesh Resort

Aloha On The Ganges is your one-stop destination for clicking mesmerizing photos. Apart from that, it has a jaw-dropping spa and other wonderful services that you will love. It’s probably the best place to cherish your memories.

10. Divine Resort: Hotel & Resort In Rishikesh

The divine resort makes an ideal location to travel with your loved one to click some magnificent pictures. It offers services like any other top hotel. The infrastructure, facilities, food everything is nothing but commendable.
