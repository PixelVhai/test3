Check List of Auspicious Wedding Dates for 2022

Getting married during a pandemic situation is nobody’s original dream. Going from planning every small detail of the big day with perfection to marrying your love in the backyard can be the most unusual thing ever. It is no secret that we Indians love planning our wedding for all the right reasons, after all, this is the biggest day of a person’s life.

There are weddings and there are Indian weddings! Apart from the singing and dancing that takes place in the wedding activities, traditional Indian weddings are filled with a huge burst of colors, and the emotions surrounding the precious Indian cultures and customs make it a truly distinguishable affair.

And one of the first steps for planning your wedding is finding an ideal wedding date that is also known as ‘Mahurat’, that can be usually described as the auspicious time to get married based on you and your partner’s horoscopes.

In the case of Hindu weddings, the families of the to-be-bride and the to-be-groom come together to find an auspicious date and time to conduct the wedding ceremony, based on Vedic astrological information. As a bride and a grown step into new beginnings, it is always considered to get married on the decided time, for navigating a seamless life ahead.
