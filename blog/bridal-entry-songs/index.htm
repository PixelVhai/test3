10+ Lit Hindi Songs that are Best for a Bride’s Entry

Home » Blog » 10+ Lit Hindi Songs that are Best for a Bride’s Entry

Author: Meenakshi Tyagi

March 18, 2021 - in
  • Share
  • 39

If you are a bride or someone related to a bride looking for awesome Bollywood songs that could be played during the bride’s entrance, this article is just for you.

Advertisement

We all know that DJs play the most boring and old-school songs during the entrance of the bride. They neither suit your personality, nor the theme of your wedding. It is better to decide on a song that suits your personality and co-relates to the theme of your wedding.

Every eye turns towards the bride when she makes her entrance. In fact, the entry of the bride is a moment that everyone wishes to witness.

Advertisement

So, why not make this moment even more memorable by playing the perfect Bollywood song?

Depending on how you wish to make an entrance, we have segregated the list into three parts.

  • The first is a sweet and romantic entrance. We call it the ‘Sweet Heart entrance’.
  • The second is all about the entrance of a bride who knows she is glamorous and beautiful and wishes everyone to know it. Let’s call it the ‘Charming entrance’.
  • Last but not least is the confident and rocking entrance, where the bride enters while dancing to a song that compels everyone to shake a leg. We have named it the ‘Diva entrance’.
  • Let us now move to our list of amazing songs and for your convenience, we are also providing a link to the Youtube video of that song.

  1. Din Shagna Da – Phillauri
  2. Teri Ore – Singh Is Kinng
  3. Hasi (Female Version) – Hamari Adhuri Kahani
  4. Mile Ho Tum Humko.. – Neha Kakkar

Bollywood Entry Songs For Sweet Heart Entrance

This is the kind of entrance that every groom likes the most because the song that plays in the background shows the love a bride feels for her ‘about to be husband’.

1. Din Shagna Da

 

No list for a sweetheart entrance would ever be complete without “Din Shagna Da” track from the movie Phillauri. This is a soulful track that expresses the joy of a female when she finds her soulmate and it is perfect for your entrance. You can make an entrance as soon as you hear the music play.

The lyrics start quickly. Hearing the song through this link would give you a better idea. This song is loved by most of the brides and you should definitely consider this.

2. Teri Ore – Singh Is Kinng

Teri Ore is a beautiful compilation from the movie ‘Singh Is Kinng’ and is sung by none other than Shreya Ghoshal and Rahat Fateh Ali Khan. The DJ can start the song just before you are about to enter and as soon as you hear the lyrics “Rishta naya Rabba dil chhu raha hai, kheenche mujhe koi dor…”, you can make your romantic entrance and the lyrics will be “Teri ore, teri ore, teri ore, haye Rabba…” till you reach your man.

Wouldn’t it be romantic? Think about it!

3. Hasi (Female Version) – Hamari Adhuri Kahani

This is a soulful track from the movie ‘Humari Adhuri Kahani’ and the female version by Shreya Ghoshal starts with “Mai jaan ye vaar du…” This song is so perfect that you can enter as the song starts. However, when the lyrics reach “haan hasi ban gaye…” that’s when it would be perfect to make an appearance.

4. Mile Ho Tum Humko..

This track is from the movie fever. The original track is sung by Tony Kakkar. However, you should be looking for the Reprise version by Neha Kakkar. The song starts with music followed by the lyrics “Mile ho tum humko…”. You can make an entrance when these lines are repeated for the second time. Your groom would be looking at you by the time the lyrics “… churaya hai tumko kismat ki lakeeron se” would be playing in the background.

Hindi Bridal Entry Songs For Charming Entrance

The charming entrance is for the bride who wants to make her entrance only about herself. She is glam, charming, beautiful, and she knows it all.

5. Afreen Afreen – Coke Studio Season 9

This is an amazing melody for a bride who wants to show the world how beautiful she actually is. The original track is by Nusrat Fateh Ali Khan but we are talking about the track by Rahat Fateh Ali Khan. The lyrics start with “Aisa dekha nahi khoobsurat koi…” You can make an entrance as soon as the lyrics start. However “Husn-e-jana ki tareef…”

6. Tune Maari Entry – Gunday

This energetic track from the movie ‘Gunday’ is the perfectly suited song for the entry of a bride. The best time to make an appearance through this song is after you hear the sound “… tung tung tung…” at least fifteen times. As soon as you will make an appearance, everyone will go “Tune mari entry…”

7. Jag Ghumeya – Sultan

This is a song full of praise from the movie ‘Sultan’ by Rahat Fateh Ali Khan. You can make your enigmatic entrance before the words “Jag ghumeya thare…” start resounding around the guests.

8. Diva Entrance

This type of entrance is totally for the bride who knows how to groove and wants to surprise the groom with her moves.

9. London Thumakda – Queen

This song is from the movie ‘Queen’. Make a dancing appearance with this song and compel everyone to join in automatically. The best part about this track is that it is Punjabi and anyone, young or old can join in effortlessly.

Lyrics:

Tu ho gayi one to two
Oh kudiye what to do
Oh ho gayi munde di
Tu turu turu turu

Patangaan vargi
Tu ainvai udd di
Oh ho gayi munde di
Tu turu turu turu…..

 

10. Mainu lehenga le de mahanga – Jass Manak

This is an energetic Punjabi number by Jass Manak. You can make an appearance and direct every action and emotion towards the groom. Make sure you have memorized the lyrics and know the meaning of every word otherwise you will miss the naughty emotions present in the song.

11. Makhna – Drive

This is a romantic dance number from the movie ‘Drive’. The lyrics “… mai chhod aai gharbaar mera…” make this song perfect for the wedding. You can dance alone or with your girl gang on this song.

12. Saiyyan Superstar – Ek Paheli Leela

A list of bridal entry dance (Hire Top Rated Delhi NCR Choreographers for wedding, sangeet or mehendi day) is not complete if we don’t include this amazing song from the movie ‘Ek Paheli Leela’ sung by Tulsi Kumar. In this song, the bride proudly acknowledges that her husband is one of a kind. It is suggested that you give a solo performance on this dance and look more at the audience instead of just looking at your groom.

here are many songs in Bollywood. However, most of them have already been used in many marriages and this list was an attempt to bring the most unique and the most preferred songs together.
