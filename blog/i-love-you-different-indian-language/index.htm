I Love You In 25 Different Indian Languages

Author: Alka Singh

February 21, 2021 - in
  • Share
  • 39

When the song “Ti Amo” from the movie “Dum Maaro Dum” came, it not just soothes our heart but our soul. The Italian version of “I Love You” as “Ti Amo” has been on our tongue since then.

Advertisement

Even the song “I Love You” from the movie “Bodyguard” reignites the sense of charm and beauty that love brings to life.

This Valentines’ day, if you want to surprise your bae and wanna make her/him feel special, why not express your deepest feelings in your local language. India is blessed with different beautiful languages that are spoken throughout the country. Make a lasting impression on your partner by professing your love in 25 Indian languages.

Advertisement

Why?

Because “words are powerful.”

list of how to say I LOVE YOU in different languages in India

1. I Love You in Hindi

ma͠i tum se pyār kartā hū̃

2. I Love You in Bengali

Āmi tōmāẏa bhālōbāsi

7. I Love You in Urdu

  4. Main Tumse Pyar Ker Ta Hu’

main tor se maya karatho

17. I Love You in Magadhi/Magahi

19. I Love You in Awadhi

hum tohka mayaait he.
